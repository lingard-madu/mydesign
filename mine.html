AFRICAN COUNTRIES

nigeria flag

Nigeria republic in western Africa, with a coast along the Atlantic Ocean on the Gulf of Guinea. Most of Nigeria consists of a low plateau cut by rivers, especially the Niger and itslargest tributary, the Benue. The country takes its name from its chief river. Until 1991, the capital was the largest city, Lagos on the southwestern coast; at that time, the city of Abuja in the country's interior became capital.
